Food Group Home Page

This is the online home of the Transition Cambridge Food group. The group has several projects which you can find the specific below. You can get involved with the whole group, or direclty pick one of the projects below!

CamBake

contacts: Ivan

CamBake is for people in Cambridge that love bread - not taste-less, soul-less industrial bread but bread that still remembers the oven it was born in, made locally - from the finest locally produced ingredients.

Get involved! »

Cambridge CropShare

contacts: Helen

Transition Cambridge CropShare is a small-scale community-supported agriculture (CSA) scheme in Cambridge. It experiments with ‘crop-sharing,’ which we define as an exchange of use of land for access to labor - creating opportunities for farm-community collaboration.

Get involved! »

Grow Your Own Vegetables

contacts: Dave F

Why not try your hand at raising and eating fruit and vegetables with the assistance and encouragement of like-minded people who have done it before? The sessions are allotment based providing access to your own growing area, practical demonstrations and, just as important, the chance to share your successes and frustrations.

contacts: Ann M

Do you have space in your Cambridge garden which is under used? Or do you want to grow your own veg but don't have a garden to do it in? If so, the Transition Cambridge Garden Share scheme is for you!.
